Infinite Scroll

We all know what infinite scroll means. This directive does it.

Usage

<div infinite-scroll="myHandlerFunction"
    scroll-threshold="700"></div>

scroll-threshold is optional, and defaults to 200.

Waiting for content

When working with asynchronous data, the directive will often be applied too fast, before the data is rendered, and the various height calculations and whatnot will be off.

In those cases, use the wait-for-event attribute, and emit an event from your controller whenever you're done:

<div infinite-scroll="myHandlerFunction" wait-for-event="contentLoaded"></div>

// from your controller
$scope.$emit('contentLoaded');
